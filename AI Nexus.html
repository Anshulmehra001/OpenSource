A Note on Responsible AI Use

AI is a powerful tool, but it's not infallible. Always fact-check AI-generated information, especially statistics and critical data. Be mindful of potential biases in AI outputs and strive for originality by adding your own unique perspective. Use these tools to augment your intelligence, not replace it. Human oversight is the key to creating high-quality, ethical, and valuable work.
